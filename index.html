FAQ

What is Corporate Time

Corporate Time is a unique and innovative time zone initiative designed specifically for the corporate environment. It introduces a 5-minute time subtraction, allowing meetings to start right on time, eliminating the common delay of starting 5 minutes late.

What are the benefits of using Corporate Time?

By adopting Corporate Time, companies can experience several benefits:

  1. Punctuality: Meetings can start on time, improving productivity and efficiency in the workplace.
  2. Streamlined schedules: With meetings starting promptly, there is less wasted time waiting for latecomers, leading to better time management.
  3. Culture change: Embracing Corporate Time can encourage a cultural shift toward punctuality and respect for others' time.
  4. Increased productivity: By utilizing the full meeting duration, participants can make the most of their time and accomplish more during work hours.

Can individuals and companies outside the corporate world use Corporate Time?

Although Corporate Time was initially designed with corporate environments in mind, individuals and organizations outside the corporate world are free to adopt this time zone initiative if they find it beneficial. The principles behind Corporate Time, such as promoting punctuality and efficiency, can apply to various contexts. Whether it's for personal time management or other professional settings, the concept of subtracting 5 minutes to start on time can be adapted to meet specific needs.

How can we implement Corporate Time in our organization?

Implementing Corporate Time in your organization is relatively straightforward:

  1. Awareness: Educate employees about the concept of Corporate Time and the benefits it offers.
  2. Adjust clocks and schedules: Make the necessary adjustments to clocks, calendars, and meeting schedules by subtracting 5 minutes from the regular time.
  3. Communication: Inform employees, clients, and stakeholders about the implementation of Corporate Time to ensure everyone is aware of the change.
  4. Reinforce punctuality: Encourage employees to respect the new start times and reinforce the importance of punctuality.

What happens if someone arrives at a meeting at the regular start time?

If someone arrives at a meeting at the regular start time without accounting for Corporate Time, they may find that the meeting has already begun. It's important to inform all participants about the transition to Corporate Time to avoid confusion and ensure everyone adjusts their schedules accordingly.

Can Corporate Time be used in different time zones?

Yes, Corporate Time can be implemented in any time zone. The 5-minute time subtraction remains consistent regardless of the geographical location. Companies operating across multiple time zones can adopt Corporate Time to promote punctuality and consistency in their meetings.
