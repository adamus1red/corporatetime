FAQ

What is Corporate Time

Corporate Time is an innovative time zone initiative designed specifically for corporate environments. It introduces a unique concept of subtracting 5 minutes from the traditional time, enabling meetings and appointments to start promptly at the scheduled time. By implementing Corporate Time, companies can eliminate the common practice of starting meetings late and encourage a more punctual and efficient work culture.

How does Corporate Time work?

Corporate Time operates by subtracting 5 minutes from the traditional time. For example, if the standard time is 2:00 PM, Corporate Time would be 1:55 PM. This time adjustment allows meetings and other corporate activities to begin promptly, eliminating the need for the usual 5-minute delay that often occurs. By adopting Corporate Time, organizations can maximize productivity, minimize wasted time, and promote a culture of timeliness.

Can individuals and companies outside the corporate world use Corporate Time?

Although Corporate Time was initially designed with corporate environments in mind, individuals and organizations outside the corporate world are free to adopt this time zone initiative if they find it beneficial. The principles behind Corporate Time, such as promoting punctuality and efficiency, can apply to various contexts. Whether it's for personal time management or other professional settings, the concept of subtracting 5 minutes to start on time can be adapted to meet specific needs.
